Flex Ltd.[2] (previously known as Flextronics International Ltd. or Flextronics) is an American multinational technological manufacturer. It is the third[3] largest global electronics manufacturing services (EMS), original design manufacturer (ODM) company by revenue, behind only Taiwan's Pegatron to original equipment manufacturers. The corporate headquarters are located in Singapore and administrative in San Jose, California.[4][5][6] The company has manufacturing operations in over 40 countries, totalling approximately 200,000 employees.
